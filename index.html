About Me

Hello there! I'm Triston, a passionate web developer with a love for crafting user-friendly and visually appealing websites. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, I bring both creativity and technical expertise to the table.

My journey as a web developer began with creating my first Tumblr page. I loved the idea of customizing my own theme and building a space for others to visit and interact with. In University while studying Psychology, I took an Intro to Web Development course as an elective and fell in love. Since then, I have been on a thrilling ride of continuous learning and growth, always seeking out new challenges and opportunities to expand my skill set.

I believe that the key to successful web development lies in understanding the needs of users. My goal is to create seamless and intuitive experiences that leave a positive impact on visitors. Whether it's building a responsive website, optimizing performance, or diving into the realm of accessibility, I'm always up for the task.

I thrive in collaborative environments, valuing effective communication and teamwork. Working closely with designers, UX experts, and fellow developers allows me to bring projects to life with precision and efficiency.

In addition to my technical prowess, I am a firm believer in lifelong learning. I stay abreast of the latest trends, tools, and best practices in web development, ensuring that my work stays ahead of the curve.

Let's create something remarkable together!
